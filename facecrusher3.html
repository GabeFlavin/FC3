The Facecrusher 3

The "Face Crusher" horror-comedy series features a terrifying masked killer who appears every Halloween to murder his victims in a uniquely brutal way: by crushing their faces with random everyday objects. From televisions and beer kegs to disco balls and even body parts, no item is too ordinary for his gruesome killings.

Blending shocking violence with dark humor, the series delivers over-the-top kills while playing with reality. Each story ends ambiguously, leaving viewers to wonder whether the terror was real or just a nightmare. But one thing remains clear: once you've heard the legend of the Face Crusher, your fate may already be sealed.
